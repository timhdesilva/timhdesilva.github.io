Welcome!

I will be a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research (SIEPR) starting in July 2024, and will be joining the Stanford Graduate School of Business as in Assistant Professor of Finance in July 2025.

I will receive a Ph.D. in Finance from the MIT Sloan School of Management in May 2024. Prior to my Ph.D., I received a B.A. in Mathematics and Financial Economics from Claremont McKenna College and worked at Morgan Stanley and Analytic Investors.

My research is broadly within economics and finance, with a focus on the following areas:

  • Household Finance
  • Macro-Finance
  • Public Finance
  • Behavioral Economics
  • Computational Methods
