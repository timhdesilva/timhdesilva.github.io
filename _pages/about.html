Welcome!

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Finance at the Stanford Graduate School of Business. You can find my Stanford faculty page here.

I received a Ph.D. in Finance from the MIT Sloan School of Management in 2024. Prior to my Ph.D., I received a B.A. in Mathematics and Financial Economics from Claremont McKenna College and worked at Morgan Stanley and Analytic Investors.

My research is broadly within economics and finance, with a focus on the following areas:

  • Household Finance
  • Macroeconomics
  • Public Finance
  • Behavioral Economics
  • Asset Pricing
