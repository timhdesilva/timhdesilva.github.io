Welcome!

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research (SIEPR) for the 2024-25 academic year, and will be joining the Stanford Graduate School of Business as in Assistant Professor of Finance in July 2025.

I recieved a Ph.D. in Finance from the MIT Sloan School of Management. Prior to my Ph.D., I received a B.A. in Mathematics and Financial Economics from Claremont McKenna College and worked at Morgan Stanley and Analytic Investors.

My research is broadly within economics and finance, with a focus on the following areas:

  • Household Finance
  • Macro-Finance
  • Public Finance
  • Behavioral Economics
  • Computational Methods
