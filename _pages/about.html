Welcome! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Finance at the MIT Sloan School of Management. Prior to my Ph.D., I received a B.A. in Mathematics and Financial Economics from Claremont McKenna College and worked at Morgan Stanley and Analytic Investors.

My research interests include household finance, macro-finance, behavioral economics, and public finance.
